FAQ'S



Do I need a special pot for recipes?

You do not need any special equipment; you can make our recipes with your home pot. Also, specifically indicated in the recipe when necessary.

Can I double the recipe?

You can double the recipe by doubling the ingredients.

I don’t want to use oil; what do you suggest using to substitute oil in your recipes?

It depends on the recipe. You can use different things instead of oil, but you may not get the desired result in taste.

Can I share your recipes on other sites?

You can share as long as you give references.

Can we upload our own recipes to your site?

We do not have such a feature for now, but we are planning.

Can I find recipes from world cuisine?

Yes, you can, we bring you together with all the world cuisine according to your materials. We also have a goal of doubling our recipes every month.

Can I use margarine in place of butter?

Probably not. It’s too watery and doesn’t behave like butter. I would suggest vegan butter such as Earth Balance sticks rather than margarine, but real butter is all I bake with when I call for butter in my recipes and using anything else can be a big gamble.

Can I make this recipe vegan?

Yes, you can. All recipes are tageed according to diet type. The recipes that is tagged as vegan contains only vegan ingredients.
